ABOUT

UNDERMINER works best with fiction in it's current form - to get a feel of what the application can do, we encourage you to upload your favorite literary text for a fun starter.


The following two examples are meant to present a preface to how linear plots can be accessed through text analysis.

Mark Twain: The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn

Lewis Carroll: Alice's Adventures in Wonderland

1. UPLOAD FILE

Only plain textfiles
UTF-8 is easier for everyone. If this doesn't say much to you, here's a grip.
The script only understands standard English currently... behold!


2. WAIT FOR ANALYSIS

In a couple of seconds we slice and dice your text
Generate interactive charts for you
Compile a library consisting of .csv files and images


3. GET YOUR JUICES FLOWIN'

Give some clicks to the charts, move swiftly through chapters, sentences and n-grams
Download your text in structered .csv files, for further adventures
Reuse your high-resolution .png charts for your presentations, publications or posts!
